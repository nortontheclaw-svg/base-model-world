About

Cars look better when the noise is stripped out.

White Over Steel is AI-assisted automotive photography built around base-model clarity: white studio space, black trim, pressed steel, and no warm gloss.

Hero shots begin in Gemini, then angles are developed through Figma Weave until the set feels consistent: front, side, rear, and the one image that makes the car hold still.

No showroom theatre. No luxury shorthand. White over steel.
