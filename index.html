About

Base Model World is a car photography project built around a simple conviction: the best way to see a car is without distraction.

Each image begins as a 3/4-front hero shot generated with AI, then developed into three angles — dead side, dead front, rear 3/4 — using Figma Weave. The result is a consistent studio look: white infinity cove, pressed steel wheels, black bumper sections. No location. No filter. No alloy.

The aesthetic is deliberate. White over steel.
